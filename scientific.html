Chemical Depositions of Thin Films

View all

ALD ATOMIC LAYER DEPOSITION

ALD is an advanced thin film manufacturing process for mainstream applications expanding beyond semiconductor processing. It achieves new levels of performance in Li-ion batteries, fuel cells, and memory devices.

CSD CHEMICAL SOLUTION DEPOSITION

CSD is a growth technique for thin films, self-assembled nanomaterials, or nanocomposite thin films where chemical solution is deposited and transformed into solid compounds.

CVD CHEMICAL VAPOUR DEPOSITION

CVD is a chemical process used to produce high-quality, high-performance materials. Volatile precursors react and decompose on the substrate surface to create the desired thin film.
