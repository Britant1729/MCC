Welcome to MCC

The Mission Coordination Cell (MCC) is a dedicated unit established at IIT Kanpur under the National Quantum Mission (NQM). We facilitate the coordination of activities across quantum technology verticals, connecting technical hubs, ministries, and national agencies to advance India's quantum ecosystem.

Our role includes overseeing proposal evaluations, fostering collaborations, and ensuring the successful deployment of quantum technologies for societal benefit.
