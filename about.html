About MCC

The Mission Coordination Cell (MCC) serves as the central coordinating agency for the National Quantum Mission (NQM), approved by the Government of India. Hosted at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur), MCC works in close collaboration with the Department of Science and Technology (DST) and the Mission Secretariat.

Our Objectives

  • Facilitate connections between Technical Hubs (T-Hubs) in four key verticals: Quantum Computing, Quantum Communication, Quantum Sensing & Metrology, and Quantum Materials & Devices.
  • Oversee the evaluation of funding proposals through the Mission Technology Research Council (MTRC).
  • Promote awareness, training, and deployment of quantum technologies across industries and academia.
  • Ensure seamless coordination with national agencies, ministries, and international partners.

Established in 2024, MCC plays a pivotal role in realizing India's vision for quantum supremacy and technological self-reliance.
